== Description ==

If you produce original news or entertainment content, you can tap into one of the most technologically advanced traffic exchanges among blogs! Start using our Blog Promotion plugin on your site and receive 150%-300% extra traffic free! 
Idea is simple - the more traffic you send to us, the more we can send you back. Please be aware that sites which are spammy, have illegal content, or are overtly pornographic are not accepted.
The plugin generates widget which is placed on some good spot so that your visitors could see the previews of fellow bloggers posts presented in neat small boxes with descriptions.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the folder blog-promotion to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
2. Activate the plugin Blog Promotion through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Add the widget to your sidebar from Appearance->Widgets and configure the widget options.
4. Go to Settings > Blog Promotion and configure settings of the plugin.

After plugin is activated a banner will appear after the content of the post or on the sidebar.
